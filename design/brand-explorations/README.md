# Brand Explorations — Pass 1.7 Warm Academic

Three palette + type directions for Puheo's brand refresh. All three share:
- Warm accent (terracotta, mustard, or coral) — one per direction, used sparingly
- Red reserved for error states only
- Serif headline paired with sans body
- WCAG AA on all text/background pairs

Open each `preview.html` in a browser to see the live mockup.

---

## Directions

### [01 · Biblioteca](01-biblioteca/README.md) — Dark navy + terracotta
Deep ink-navy background. Terracotta CTA. Playfair Display headlines + Source Sans 3 body.

Best for: premium study-tool feel. The serif + dark combo is the strongest "not AI slop" signal.

**Preview:** `01-biblioteca/preview.html`

---

### [02 · Cuaderno](02-cuaderno/README.md) — Light parchment + mustard (LIGHT MODE)
Warm cream/parchment background — the only light-mode option. Mustard gold accent. Crimson Pro headlines + Nunito body.

Best for: parent-trust signal, accessibility, differentiation from dark-mode competitors.

**Preview:** `02-cuaderno/preview.html`

---

### [03 · Alhambra](03-alhambra/README.md) — Dark charcoal + muted coral
Very dark warm brown background. Muted coral CTA. Left-aligned editorial layout. Cormorant Garamond headlines + DM Sans body.

Best for: strongest personality, most opinionated, Mediterranean culture reference without cliché.

**Preview:** `03-alhambra/preview.html`

## Trade-off summary

| | Biblioteca | Cuaderno | Alhambra |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mode | Dark | **Light** | Dark |
| Accent | Terracotta | Mustard | Coral |
| Headline serif | Playfair Display | Crimson Pro | Cormorant Garamond |
| Body sans | Source Sans 3 | Nunito | DM Sans |
| "Parent trust" signal | Strong (serious) | **Strongest** | Medium |
| Visual differentiation | High | **Highest** (light) | High |
| Brand personality | Restrained/academic | Warm/approachable | Dramatic/cultural |
| Implementation risk | Low | Medium (light mode sweep) | Medium (serif fragility) |

**Recommendation:** Biblioteca (01) is the safest and most scalable choice — the navy-terracotta combo works across all app contexts (dark exercise screens, dark cards, dark modals) without requiring a light/dark mode strategy decision. Cuaderno is the right choice if Marcel wants to stand out from every other dark-mode study app and is willing to do the light-mode token sweep. Alhambra is the right choice if brand personality matters more than conservatism.
